Connect Redox

Redox is a healthcare integration aggregator that exposes ~200 EHRs (Practice Fusion, Greenway, NextGen, AdvancedMD, athenahealth, and others) via a single FHIR R4 API. Connect once at the organization level — every member of your org will then be able to import patient demographics from any covered EHR via MRN lookup.
